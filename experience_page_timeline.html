Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.), Mechanics

Ecole Nationale Supérieure d’Arts et Métiers, LISPEN Laboratory, Lille, France

Before moving to Paris, I spent 3.5 years in Lille working on my PhD in mechanics, specializing in nonlinear dynamics of flexible structures. I was lucky enough to have been a part of the Marie Curie program THREAD-ETN, which funded a cornucopia of international trainings and conferences.

I defended my thesis titled "Nonlinear dynamics of highly flexible slender beams: efficient numerical strategies in the frequency domain" and under the direction of Pr. Olivier THOMAS on 13 December 2023. It is available at (link to theses . fr).

August 2019 - May 2020
